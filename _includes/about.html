About

Independent Record Label based at Fres Wota 4, Efate, Vanuatu

  • 2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We initially started out as a mobile recording studio to record upcoming Ni-Van artists.

  • April 2012

    Pilo Wan Plus Four

    Our first project was "Pilo Wan Plus Four - Nasama Resort" and since then we have made it our mission to help struggling artists.

  • April 2013

    Manples Warriors

    We launched this project back in 2012, to help youth at Manples Area through music. Around April of 2013 the boys came together and formed Manples Warriors Band.

  • December 2013

    Independent Record Label

    When asked, what is the biggest obstacle in our music industry? The answer is not enough exposure on the International Market. So we move into publishing to kick start promotion of Vanuatu Artists and Producers.

  • July 2014

    Paxton Beatz Compilations Vol:1

    Paxton Beatz is a young talented producer and beat maker. He is also one of the main driving force behind Kings Tone 4 Records.

  • October 2014

    Mobile App

    Now you can get the latest music from Vanuatu on your mobile device. Support for both iOS and Android devices.

  • November 2014

    Website Redesign

    Beautiful, fast, clean website design using flask micro framework and bootstrap.
